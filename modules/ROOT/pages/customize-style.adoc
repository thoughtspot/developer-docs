Log in to ThoughtSpot as an admin or a developer user.\n. To access the ThoughtSpot Developer portal, click *Develop*\n. Under *Customizations*, click *Styles*.\n\n[NOTE]\n====\nStyle customization settings are also available in the ThoughtSpot *Admin* tab.\nFor more information on setting UI styles from the *Admin* tab, see link:https://docs.thoughtspot.com/cloud/latest/style-customization[Style Customization, window=_blank].\n====\n\n[#logo-change]\n== Customize application logo and favicon\nTo upload your application logos:\n\n. To access the ThoughtSpot Developer portal, click *Develop*\n. Under *Customizations*, click *Styles*.\n. To update fonts for charts, go to *Chart Visualization Fonts*, and select a chart label.\n.
